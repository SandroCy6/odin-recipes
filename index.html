Causa Rellena

This layered Peruvian potato and chicken salad is called "causa rellena" in Peru. There's nothing very unusual about serving a chicken or potato salad at a cookout but this beautiful layered salad is sure to cause a stir! Perfect for making ahead and packing in your cooler on a hot summer day. Garnish with cherry tomatoes and cilantro if desired.

Peruvian Arroz con Pollo

This is a Peruvian rice and chicken dish made with cilantro and a special Peruvian kick. It's very delicious. My Peruvian father-in-law said that mine was even better than his sister's, who is one of the best cooks in the family (besides my mother-in-law)! Goes great with Huancaina sauce and salsa Criolla.

Sweet and Sour Meatballs (Suan T'ien Niu Jou Po Lo La Tzu)

I grew up in Japan, and my Mother got this recipe from some Catholic nuns there during the 50's. It's been a family favorite ever since. If you're a vegetarian, try this sauce with vegetables only. It's wonderful that way also!
